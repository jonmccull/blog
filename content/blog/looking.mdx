I’m looking for my next Product Marketing role 🚀

Over the years I’ve worked with:

- niche web browsers taking on the giants,
- B2B SaaS fighting for product-market fit,
- productivity apps that run the lives of millions.

Each case had a different target market, audience, and GTM motion.

So, what stayed the same?

Product marketing sat in a unique space between Marketing, Customer Success, and Product. An orchestrater. A connector tackling complex, cross-functional work.

And it’s exactly this unique position in the org chart that keeps me pumped about the craft, and how it will evolve through the current moment (👋 🤖).

Sitting in that “whitespace” of a company as a product marketer means you get to tackle fresh problems daily. And through, for example, positioning, messaging and intel - work on things that have real downstream impacts for a business and the people that use your products.

What excites me most, though, is the future of product marketing. The groundwork is being laid for totally new ways of collaborating and redefining what we even mean by “knowledge work” and SaaS. We’re all creating workflows, spinning-up foundation docs, and planning hand-offs between hyper-specialized agents.

There’s never been a better time to work in the “whitespace”. 😁
